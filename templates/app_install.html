INSTALL THE APP
1
Open Chrome browser
Visit app.ranking.worldstrongman.org in Google Chrome on your Android device.
2
Tap the menu (⋮)
Tap the three dots in the top right corner of Chrome.
3
Add to Home screen
Tap "Add to Home screen" and confirm. The WSM app icon will appear on your home screen.
4
Open and sign in
Tap the WSM icon on your home screen and sign in with your credentials.
1
Open Safari browser
Visit app.ranking.worldstrongman.org in Safari (not Chrome) on your iPhone or iPad.
2
Tap Share button
Tap the Share icon (box with arrow) at the bottom of Safari.
3
Add to Home Screen
Scroll down and tap "Add to Home Screen". Give it a name and tap Add.
4
Open and sign in

Open Chrome or Edge

3

Click "Install" in the popup. The app opens in its own window like a native app.
