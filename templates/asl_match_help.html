ASL MATCH GUIDE
How to enter results during a match
Before the match
During the match
After the match
— Before the match —
1
Find your match
Go to ASL → find your division → Matches tab. Find your match by round and date. Click PROTOCOL to open the result entry screen.
ASL → Division → Matches → PROTOCOL
2
Check your side
The system shows which side you judge. You only see the input for your side — the other judge enters the other side.

🏠 HOME SIDE — enter home athlete results

✈️ AWAY SIDE — enter away athlete results
Shown in MATCH PROTOCOL header
— During the match —
3
Enter discipline results
For each discipline — enter the numeric result and press SAVE. The winner is determined automatically. The organizer selects 5 disciplines from 8 standard WSM Battle disciplines when creating the match:
DisciplineUnitRule
Stone Flipreps⬆ higher wins
Dumbbell Liftreps⬆ higher wins
Axle Deadliftreps⬆ higher wins
Log Liftreps⬆ higher wins
Husafell Sandbag Carrysec⬇ lower wins
Super Yokesec⬇ lower wins
Farmer's Walksec⬇ lower wins
Tire Flipssec / m⬇ lower wins
Input field → SAVE per discipline
4
Score updates automatically
After each SAVE the match score at the top updates immediately.

Home 2 : 1 Away


Score = disciplines won. Equal result = DRAW (each side gets 0.5).
Score header — updates live
— After the match —
5
Confirm final score
When all disciplines are entered — the organizer confirms the final score via the RESULT button on the match card. This marks the match as COMPLETED and updates the standings.
Organizer: Match card → RESULT button
6
Print protocol
The organizer or admin can print the official match protocol — includes team logos, all discipline results, winner column and signature lines for both judges and the organizer.
🖨 PRINT button (admin/organizer only)
