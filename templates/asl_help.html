ASL ORGANIZER GUIDE
Strongman Super League — step-by-step from setup to results
League setup
Season preparation

— League Setup —
1
Access your league
The league is created by the WSM administrator. You receive access to the league as an organizer. Open ASL in the cabinet to see your league and its divisions.
ASL in navigation menu
2
Create divisions
Inside the league create divisions — weight categories or regional groups. Each division has its own standings table, team roster and match schedule.
League → + Add Division
— Season Preparation —
3
Add teams to division
Select teams from the global WSM database and add them to the division. The Teams tab shows all participating teams with logos and countries.
Division → Teams tab → + Add Team
4
Schedule matches
Create matches: select home and away teams, set the date and round number. Assign two judges — Judge 1 for the home athlete, Judge 2 for the away athlete. Select disciplines from the 8 standard WSM disciplines.
Division → Matches tab → + New Match
— Match Day —
5
Open match protocol
Click PROTOCOL on the match card — opens the discipline-by-discipline result entry screen. Judges enter results for their side. The match score updates automatically after each discipline.
Match card → PROTOCOL button
6
Confirm match score
After all disciplines are entered — confirm the final score via the RESULT button. Score format: disciplines won by each team (e.g. 3:2). Win = 3 pts · Draw = 1 pt · Loss = 0 pts. Standings update automatically.
Match card → RESULT button
7
Check standings
The Standings tab shows the full league table: matches played, wins, losses, disciplines won/lost, and points. Top 2 teams advance to playoffs. The public league page updates automatically.
Division → Standings tab
